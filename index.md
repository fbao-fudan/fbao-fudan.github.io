# Feng BAO 鲍峰

<p class="hero-role">Principal Investigator &nbsp;·&nbsp; College for Future Information Technology, Fudan University, Shanghai</p>

<div class="hero-chips">
  <a class="chip" href="https://baolab-fudan.github.io/index.html" target="_blank">Bao Lab ↗</a>
  <a class="chip" href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=I0mcA3MAAAAJ&hl=en" target="_blank">Google Scholar ↗</a>
  <a class="chip" href="mailto:fbao@fudan.edu.cn">fbao@fudan.edu.cn</a>
  <span class="chip">Shanghai, China</span>
</div>

My research develops statistical learning and AI methods to answer fundamental biological questions and advance medicine — spanning self-supervised representation learning, multi-modal information fusion, causal inference in complex biological systems, and deep generative models for spatial omics.

Previously: Postdoc (2019–2024) at [Altschuler and Wu Lab](https://www.altschulerwulab.org/), UCSF, and visiting scholar at [Dana-Farber Cancer Institute](https://www.dana-farber.org/), Harvard Medical School (2018–2019). PhD from [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/) (2019).
